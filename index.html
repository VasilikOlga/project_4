The next chapter in your business is about to unfold.

We noticed one day: Everybody’s got pockets. That gave us an idea: Let’s fill them with useful stuff – stuff businesses can use for marketing, stuff customers will appreciate and keep!

And ExpandaCard was born.

ExpandaCard is a premier, pocket-based, folding media solution. (Sounds fancy, doesn’t it!) It’s perfect for maps, marketing materials, menus – heck, use it for Magna Carta if you like.

ExpandaCard is a sheet of paper – any size you like! – that folds neatly down to credit card size so it fits easily anywhere on your person – now, now, get your mind out of the gutter – we meant wallets, purses and those ubiquitous pockets we mentioned earlier. It’s full colour throughout, so no matter which facing you’re looking at, you’re looking at colour.

That is, your customers are. ExpandaCard is absolutely perfect for all kinds of revenue-generating purposes. Print a tourist guide and sell some ads on it. Or a calendar showcasing monthly promotions. Or flashcards, or a football schedule or pretty much anything your wandering mind dreams up. If it can be printed, then with ExpandaCard it can be printed and folded and turned into a useful little pocket-sized thingie.

Oh, and if you’re familiar with folding media – ours is a lot nicer. ExpandaCard comes with a built-in belt that – well, it keeps the card from expanding suddenly at the wrong moment, like when you look into your lover’s eyes and lean in for a kiss, the last thing you want is BAM!, my pocket media just exploded, major buzzkill. ExpandaCard’s snazzy belt keeps your media and your message pristine and tidy, ready for its next (intentional, not surprising) expansion.

We’ve been making ExpandaCard for more than 15 years, for some of the world’s largest and most sophisticated marketers and advertising agencies. Learn more about this unique product, then by all means contact us and find out what we can do for you.
